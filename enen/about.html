About Portal

Manfaat Portal is an initiative by Agensi Pelaksanaan Ekonomi dan Koordinasi Strategik Nasional (LAKSANA) under the Ministry of Finance.

This portal was developed as a one-stop portal aimed at facilitating Rakyat to access latest information related to aid schemes and initiatives across various Ministries and Agencies efficiently, accurately, inclusively, transparently and reliably. Through this Portal, information on the desired assistance and initiatives as well as applications is possible at your fingertips at any time.

Experience the services provided by the Manfaat Portal, today.
