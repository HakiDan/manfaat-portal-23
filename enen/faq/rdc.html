Direct Selling Industry Natural Treasure Program Research, Development, and/or Commercialization (R&D&C) Grant

Open for Application

Last updated on 07/10/2022

Introduction

  • The R&D&C Grant aims to help direct selling industry players, especially direct selling companies that produce and market local products based on natural resources and help direct selling companies commercialize direct selling products.
  • The maximum grant amount (per direct sales company application) is RM300,000.
    • Helping direct selling companies business to produce and market local products that are based on high potential natural resources;
    • Helping direct selling companies enhance their commercialization for local direct selling products through digital marketing innovation or new technology development; and
    • Helping improve development and highlight the importance of local direct selling products as one of the most potential economic resources for the country.

Eligibility

  • Direct selling companies interested in applying for R&D&C Grant must meet the following conditions:
    • Have a valid direct selling license from KPDNHEP for at least two (2) years from the date of approval;
    • Must be a Malaysian direct selling company with valid registration (SSM) with minimum 70% equity ownership by Malaysians;
    • Direct selling companies must registered and be part of either Direct Selling Association of Malaysia (DSAM) or Malaysian Direct Distribution Association (MDDA) member;
    • Direct selling companies have submitted the Company Information and Performance Form (BMS) for the latest three (3) years to KPDNHEP;
    • Direct selling company is not declared bankrupt or involved in winding up proceedings;
    • Applications must be submitted through DSAM/ MDDA as well as during the advertising period. Any application after advertising period will not be accepted and processed;
    • Direct selling company can only submit one (1) application; and
    • Grant approval is subject to the decision of Jawatankuasa Pengurusan Inisiatif Pembangunan Jualan Langsung KPDNHEP (JPPJL), budget and justification of R&D&C proposal by direct selling company together with recommended by DSAM/ MDDA.
  • Applications other than direct selling companies that interested in applying for this R&D&C Grant must get fully recommendation either from DSAM/ MDDA as well as the approval of JPPJL.

    1. Application for R&D&C Grants will be implemented through advertising or direct approach method where DSAM/ MDDA will identify potential direct selling companies that have R&D&C proposals for the development of the direct selling industry
    2. Announcements on R&D&C Grants will be made by DSAM/ MDDA through the association's official portal or social media to direct selling companies and interested parties (other than direct selling companies).
    3. Application must be submitted to DSAM/MDDA before being forward to KPDNHEP for consideration.
    4. DSAM/MDDA will be in charge on identifying and execute screening process for application submitted before being recommended to KPDNHEP
