Housing Credit Guarantee Scheme (HCGS)

Open for Application

Last updated on 07/10/2022

Introduction

  • The scheme aims to help first home buyers who do not have a fixed income such as gig workers, independent business owners, and small traders or entrepreneurs.
Criteria Scheme Features
Purpose of Financing Mortgage Financing
Financing Limit Up to RM400,000.00 (including principal and MRTA*/MRTT*/LTHO*, solicitor’s fee and valuation fee)
Facility Type Term Loan/ Term Financing
Financing Tenure Up to 35 years or up to the tenure of the loan financing, whichever is earlier (two generation financing allowed)
Guarantee Coverage 100% guarantee of total financing obtained from participating Financial Institutions (FI) (including principal and MRTA*/MRTT*/LTHO*, solicitor’s fee and valuation fee)
Interest/ Profit Rate As determined by the participating FI
Deposit Mandatory deposit equivalent to 3 months monthly instalment

*MRTA: Mortgage Reducing Term Assurance
*MRTT: Mortgage Reducing Term Takaful

Eligibility

  • Malaysan citizens aged 18 years and above. Joint financing allowed
  • Purchase of first new residential house or existing house (sub-sale) or auctioned house which are under low or medium or affordable category and to be owner occupied
  • Open to newly completed or under construction or sub-sale or auctioned residential property
  • The total repayment of all applicant's loans does not exceed 65% of gross monthly income
  • CCRIS* record of arrears not more than 2 months within a period of 12 months
  • No other adverse credit records within the last 24 months

*CCRIS: Central Credit Reference Information System

Application Method

  • Overview of the process:
    1. Potential borrower identifies property. Subsequently applies and submits relevant documents to Participating Financial Institutions (PFIs)
    2. PFIs provides financing facilities to eligible borrower
    3. PFIs evaluates and reviews borrower's credit application and submits application to Syarikat Jaminan Kredit Perumahan Berhad(SJKP) for guarantee coverage, where required
    4. SJKP reviews the application and provide guarantee coverage (subject to fulfilment of terms and conditions)
    5. PFIs provides letter offer (LO) to borrower once guarantee is obtained from SJKP
    6. Borrower accepts the LO and own their dream home after FI disburse the financing
  • Documents required:
    1. Sale & Purchase Agreement/ booking sheet/ down payment receipt
    2. Latest 6-months Bank or deposit account statement (if any)
    3. Latest Income Tax Return/ EPF Statement
    4. Employment confirmation letter
    5. Copy of Identity Card of applicant(s)
    6. Employment confirmation letter (if any)
    7. Business license; or Fisherman’s registration card; or taxi permit (whichever is applicable)
    8. Declaration letter on self-employment or income stream duly confirmed by JKKK or Category A government servants, Penghulu/Ketua Kampung or Bank Branch Manager

*JKKK: Jawatankuasa Kemajuan dan Kemakmuran Kampung
