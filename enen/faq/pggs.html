PEMULIH Government Guarantee Scheme (PGGS)

Open for Application

Last updated on 07/10/2022

Introduction

  • Objective of PGGS is to provide financing assistance to Malaysian Small and Medium Enterprises ("SME") and Mid-Sized Companies ("MSC") via:
    1. New Financing (especially to large SMEs and MSC); and
    2. Restructuring of current financing facilities with moratorium and extension of tenure, as well as further funding of working capital to revitalise their business, if necessary

    Item Guarantee Scheme Terms
    Scheme Limit
    • RM30 billion (Including RM 2 billion for Bumiputera)
    Eligibility
    • Open to all entities (SMEs and MSCs) in all industries such as tourism and its related sectors with annual revenue of not more than RM500 million
    Purpose of Financing
    • Working Capital/CAPEX facilities for New Financing
    • For the restructuring of existing financing facilities and/or additional Working Capital
    • Note: The facility cannot be used to refinance existing facility granted by the same or other participating FIs
    Financing Limit
    • Working Capital/CAPEX facilities for New Financing: Up to RM20 million per company
    • Restructuring of existing financing facilities; and/or additional Working Capital: Group Exposure financing limit of up to RM50 million
    Guarantee Coverage
    • Working Capital/ CAPEX facilities for New Financing: 80% of the financing obtained from the participating FIs (including principal and interest/profit) by the Government of Malaysia
    • Restructuring of existing financing facilities:
      1. Guarantee coverage is determined as below;
        Guarantee Coverage = A X B X C, where:
        • A = 80%
        • B = 36 months (3 years)
        • C = Average monthly instalment for Year 2 and Year 3 of the restructured facility
      2. Additional working capital:
        • Up to 80% of the financing obtained from the participating Financial Institutions (including principal and interest/ profit) by the Government of Malaysia
    Guarantee Fee
    • New Financing: 1% per annum payable upfront
    • Restructuring of existing financing facilities and/ or additional working capital: 0.75% per annum payable upfront
    Tenure of Guarantee
    • New Financing: Up to 10 years
    • Restructuring of existing financing facilities: 3 years, including a 12-months moratorium
    • Additional working capital: Up to 10 years
    Type of Financing Facility
    • Term Loan/ Term Financing-i, Overdraft/ Cash Line-i, Revolving Credit/ Revolving Facility-i and Trade/ Trade-i Facility
    Participating FIs
    • Commercial Banks, Islamic Banks and Development Financial Institutions under the purview of Bank Negara Malaysia are eligible to participate in this Scheme
    Stamp Duty
    • Exemption on Stamp Duty on restructuring and/or rescheduling documentation, only up to 31 December 2022

Eligibility

  • Open to all entities (SMEs and MSCs) in all industries such as tourism and its related sectors with annual revenue of not more than RM500 million:
    1. Sole proprietorships owned by a Malaysian Citizen;
    2. Partnerships, and Limited Liability Partnerships controlled and majority owned by Malaysian Citizens; or
    3. Companies with at least 51% shares held and controlled by Malaysian Citizens
  • And are duly registered with:
    1. Companies Commission of Malaysia either under the Registration of Business Act (1956) or the Companies Act (1965/ 2016) or Limited Liability Partnership Act (2012); or
    2. Respective authorities or district offices in Sabah and Sarawak; or
    3. Respective statutory bodies for professional services providers
  • Non-Manufacturing:
    1. Companies with sales turnover not exceeding RM20.0 million OR full-time employees not exceeding 75 workers
  • Manufacturing:
    1. Companies with sales turnover not exceeding RM50.0 million OR full-time employees not exceeding 200 workers
  • Excluding:
    1. Entities that are public-listed on the main board; and
    2. Subsidaries of:
      1. Public-listed companies on the main board;
      2. Government-linked companies (GLCs);
      3. Syarikat Menteri Kewangan Diperbadankan (MKDs); and
      4. State-owned enterprises

Application Method

  • Application is opened until full utilisation of the scheme limit, except the Guarantee for restructuring and/or reshceduling which is only available until 31 December 2023.
  • SMEs to apply financing facilities from participating FIs
