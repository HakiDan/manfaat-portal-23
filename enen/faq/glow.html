MDEC GLOW – GLOW Intensive 3.0

Open for Application

Last updated on 03/11/2022

Introduction

  • Global Online Workforce (GLOW) is a programme that will equip the participants with skills and know-how to become successful digital freelancers.
  • Initiative benefit include:
    • Training Allowances worth RM 500
    • Incentives - Bidding and Platform credit worth RM 850

      • *T&C Apply

Eligibility

  • Malaysian citizen aged over 21 years old and not over 60 years old when apply for the programme

Application Method

  • Application period starts from September 2021
  • Documents needed:
    • Copy of NRIC
    • Copy or screen capture of front-page bank statement

      • *As per specified during registration

  • Application process:
    • Individuals interested in participating in the GLOW Aspiration Program and meet the minimum eligibility requirements required to register above online starting September 2021. Opening announcement registration of this program will be notified through all websites official MDEC and K-KOMM.
    • Registered candidates must also undergo a brief assessment test and will be screened in advance by the secretariat and only participants who are eligible will be accepted into the GLOW Special Program Aspiration.
    • The programme secretariat will contact the successful candidate via email/phone to set the appropriate training date (to be held online) and the selected participants must also agree and sign the AkuJanji Program Form with MDEC
