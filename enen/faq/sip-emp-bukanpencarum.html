Sistem Insurans Pekerjaan Elaun Mencari Pekerjaan+ (SIP EMP+) to non-contributors

Open for Application

Last updated on 07/10/2022

Introduction

  • SIP EMP+ to non-contributors is financial assistance given to non -SOCSO contributors, especially new graduates, school leavers and informal sector workers affected by the COVID 19 crisis to continue to survive while seeking new employment opportunities
  • SIP EMP+ assistance to non-contributors includes a Job Search Allowance (EMP) incentive of RM300 per month received when an eligible individual attends an interview and a Work Preparation Allowance (EPB) of RM400 one-off if they have returned to work

    • Job Search Activity Job Search Effort Total Payment
    Attending an open interview A minimum of 3 interview sessions for each monthly EMP payment starting 1 January 2022 / the following months RM300 per month
    Employed within the first month of receiving this incentive Attend at least one interviews in the period of receiving EMP RM300 EMP + RM400 EPB
    Employed within the second month of receiving this incentive Attend at least four interviews in the period of receiving EMP RM300 EMP + RM300 EMP + RM400 EPB
    Employed within the fourth month of receiving this incentive Attend at least seven interviews in the period of receiving EMP RM300 EMP + RM300 EMP + RM300 EMP + RM400 EPB

    Obtained a job before the date of SIP EMP+ application Attend one or more interviews and obtain a job placement before the date of application RM400 EPB

Eligibility

Eligibility Requirements for SIP EMP+ For Non-Contributors
Applicant eligibility
  • Individuals who have not been registered or are not actively contributing to SOCSO after 1 June 2021
  • Individuals who are 18 years of age and not more than 60 years of age on the date of application
  • Each applicant is required to register, complete a profile and actively create search activities on the MYFutureJobs Portal from 1 January 2022
  • Applicants must attend interview activities at least three times a month through;
    1. Open interview program organized by SOCSO is given priority. Interview information can be obtained through the MYFutureJobs Portal or;
    2. Co-organized by a registered employer and who has advertised the position on the MYFutureJobs Portal or;
    3. Externally organized and certified by a private employer/employment agency
Type of Interview
  • Applicants must attend an interview session with three (3) different employers in the same time period or on different days
  • Applicants may attend any interview arranged by the employer who has registered on MYFutureJobs Portal
  • Applicants may attend any interview that is equivalent to qualifications or appropriate to experience
Incentive Payment Period
  • EMP payment period is monthly (30 days)
  • EPB will be paid together with EMP if a new job has been offered

Application Method

  • The application period for the SIP EMP+ program to non- contributors is from 1 January 2022 to 31 December 2022
  • Documents required to apply are:
    1. Copy of Applicant's Identity Card
    2. Copy of Applicant's Own Bank Account Number Statement (not Joint account)
    3. Evidence of attending 2 interviews during each EMP, if it is not organized by SOCSO and needs confirmation from the employer who organized the interview
    4. Interview activity update in MYFutureJobs Portal Activity Log

    Application for SIP EMP+ for Non-Contributors
    Application Procedure
    • Register and complete a Profile on the MYFutureJobs Portal
    • Download and register on the Prihatin PERKESO Application (PlayStore / AppleStore) for the purpose of payment of allowances according to eligibility
    • Attend the interview program & update the activity log on the MYFutureJobs Portal (Company confirmation must be attached if attending the interview program not organized by SOCSO)
    • Update the return date to work in the Prihatin PERKESO Application if you have received a job offer for the purpose of EPB payment
    Application Status Check
    • Applicants can check the status of the application through the Prihatin PERKESO Application (PlayStore / AppleStore)
