Aerospace Matching Grant

Upcoming

Last updated on 07/10/2022

Introduction

  • The Government is offering a special grant to finance capital expenditure (CAPEX) for new machines/equipment, tooling/jigs/fixtures, first product qualification and approval as well as special certification/licensing required for Bumiputera Aerospace SMEs in procuring new work packages for aerospace manufacturing, MRO, systems integration and aerospace training activities.

  • Initiative objective:
    1. The need to strengthen the supply chain as well as to develop Bumiputera Aerospace SMEs to drive comprehensive and balanced growth in the Malaysian aerospace industry.
    2. Incentive to transfer high contract values of work packages and services.
    3. This initiative may also help Bumiputera Aerospace SMEs to grow their business, enhance Malaysian aerospace ecosystem as well as to attract new foreign investment to the country.

Eligibility

  • Applications are open to any Bumiputera Aerospace SMEs in Malaysia and have business in aerospace manufacturing and services.
  • The scope of the Grant must be within the framework of the priority areas as follows:
    1. Technical training of Malaysians for new projects
    2. R&D, which covers jigs & fixtures design fabrication, first article, raw material, engineering wages etc.
    3. CAPEX for new machinery/equipment/ tooling/ jigs & fixtures/ mould & die /IT hardware/software/ other related expenses
    4. Customer approval on processes
    5. First product qualification & approval; accreditation/ certification/licensing.

Application Method

  • Documents required:
    1. Certified True Copy (CTC) NRIC of directors/shareholders
    2. SSM search with digital certified
    3. Valid business license
    4. Certified True Copy (CTC) Manufacturing License (ML) OR a Confirmation Letter Exempted from ML from MIDA under Industrial Co-ordination Act 1975 (Applicable for manufacturing company only)
    5. Certified True Copy (CTC) License/ Permit/ Support letter from relevant ministries/ agencies that govern the activities
    6. Certified True Copy (CTC) latest six (6) months' bank statements (All banks)
    7. Latest Financial Statements (audited/management/draft)
    8. Copy of Letters of Offer for all existing banking
    9. Project proposal
    10. Profile of company/organization, key management & technical personnel, and organization chart
    11. Collaborative agreement for Public Private Partnership initiative
    12. Project milestones
    13. List of industry taker(s)
    14. List of potential partner(s)
    15. Quotation (at least 2 for pricing comparison)/ pro-forma invoice/ invoice/receipt/ purchase contract/ catalogue/ documentary evidence for related cost & expenses
    16. Other documents which you deem relevant for the project
