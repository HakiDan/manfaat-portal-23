Housewives’ Social Security Scheme (SKSSR)

Open for Application

Last updated on 09/01/2023

Introduction

  • Housewives’ Social Security Scheme (SKSSR) (SKSSR) provides protection to housewives against domestic accidents or other incidents in the course of performing their household duties and affairs
  • Housewives’ Social Security Scheme (SKSSR) (SKSSR) also protects housewives who are afflicted with illness or invalidity
  • Aimed to provide social security protection to housewives against domestic accidents and invalidity while managing the household

Eligibility

  • Eligibility:
    1. Malaysian citizens/permanent residents
    2. Housewives who have not reached the age of 55/not yet reached the age of 54 on the first date contribution made to SKSSR
    3. Advance payment of contributions, which covers a period of 12 months
  • Documents needed:
    • Click Registration & Contribution Form

Application Method

  • No application period
  • Click SKSSR Portal User Guide for further information
