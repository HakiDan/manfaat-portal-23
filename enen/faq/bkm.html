Eligibility

  • Applicants are eligible to receive BKM if they meet the following requirements:
Category Age Income (RM)
Household (Married) N/A 5,000 and below
Household (Single parents/ widow/ widower with children and are eligible for BKM) N/A
Single (Single parents/ widow/ widower without children and are eligible for BKM) 60 years old and above
Single (Single parents/ widow/ widower without children and are eligible for BKM) 21 years old - 59 years old 2,500 and below

Application Method

  • The application period is closed. However, approved recipients are able to update their basic information detail as follow:
    1. Home phone No.
    2. Mobile phone
    3. Personal email address
    4. Applicant's full name and account no.
    5. Detail on repressentative
  • Those who received BPR 2021, BKC B40 and BPR 2021 applicant whose BKM application is rejected due to receiving GKP:
    1. You will automatically be directed to BKM system to verify your eligibility
    2. You are not required to make a new BKM application
    3. Any changes on the information (if required) must be made within the specified period as stated on the BKM Portal
  • If you have never applied for BPR or BKC, and do not have an existing record in LHDNM database:
    1. You can make a new BKM application online at the BKM Portal
    2. Or visit any branch / satellite office / revenue service center / Urban Transformation Center (UTC) of the LHDNM to file and submit the BKM Application Form
  • BKM payment method is similar to the previous BPR 2021 / BKC payment method:
