12% PTPTN Loan Repayment Discount

Applications Closed

Last updated on 07/10/2022

Introduction

  • Benefits to borrowers of the National Higher Education Fund Corporation (PTPTN) through the provision of a 12% loan repayment discount incentive
  • Discount Calculation: The 12% discount is calculated based on the total repayment (at least 50% of the outstanding balance) received for the month

Note: The discount given will not reduce the monthly installment but only reduce the total debt balance

Eligibility

  • All PTPTN borrowers (including those who have been subjected to enforcement action) who make repayments of at least 50% of the total outstanding loan amount in a single payment, between 1 November 2021 to 30 April 2022
  • Borrowers who have completed a Diploma and are pursuing a Degree with the PTPTN loan, are NOT eligible for this incentive

    • For further information, please refer to 12% PTPTN Loan Repayment Discount FAQ

Application Method

To obtain the confirmation of loan balance through:

  • PTPTN Official Portal OR
  • Contact the Marketing Executive
