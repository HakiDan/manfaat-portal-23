Young Entrepreneur Fund 2.0 (YEF 2.0) Financing

Open for Application

Last updated on 07/10/2022

Introduction

  • Financing packages to help and support young entrepreneurs to grow their business to a higher level and remain viable post COVID-19 or to start a new business under SME Bank's “Umbrella initiative”

    • Features Explanation
    Financing Purpose
  • Plant / machinery / equipment acquisitions
  • Building acquisitions and / or renovations
  • Working Capital requirements including financing of guarantee fee, documentation and stamp duty fees, and Takaful contribution (if applicable)
    • Financing Concept
  • Commodity Murabahah Term Financing-i (CMTF-i)
  • Revolving Credit-i
  • Cash Line-i
  • Ijarah Thummal Bai’ Term Financing (ITB)
    		•
    Financing Limit
  • Minimum : RM100,000
  • Maximum : RM1,000,000
    		•
    Margin of Financing
  • Plant/ Machinery/ Equipment
    1. Brand New : Up to 80%
    2. Used / Refurbish / Recond : Up to 70%
  • Buiding Acquisitions : Up to 90%
  • Working Capital : Up to 100%
  • Building Renovations
    1. Own Premise : Up to 70% of the renovation cost
    2. Rented Premise : Up to 50% of the renovation cost
    Financing Tenure
  • Plant/ Machinery/ Equipment : Up to 10 years
  • Buiding Acquisitions : Up to 10 years
  • Working Capital : Up to 5 years
  • Building Renovations
    1. Own Premise : Up to 7 years
    2. Rented Premise : Up to 5 years
    Profit Rate
  • Year 1 - Year 7 : 5.0% fixed per annum (net of profit rate subsidy of 1.5% per annum)
  • Year 8 - Year 10 : 6.5% fixed per annum
    • Collateral & Takaful
  • Any collateral acceptable by the Bank as per SME Bank policy
  • Guarantee from Syarikat Jaminan Pembiayaan Perniagaan (SJPP) is required for this financing
  • Personal guarantees / joint and several guarantees
  • Takaful/insurance coverage is mandatory if the asset is given as security for the financing (if applicable). You have the option to select the service offered by our panel Takaful operators or non-panel Takaful operators
    		•

Eligibility

  • Malaysian young entrepreneurs between 21 - 40 years of age from date of application and owning a business
  • Micro, small and medium enterprises (MSMEs) as defined by SME Corp
  • Registered with Suruhanjaya Syarikat Malaysia (SSM), or authorities / district offices (for Sabah and Sarawak), or statutory bodies for professional service providers
  • For a partnership firm or a Sdn Bhd company, the youth applicant must hold majority shares of >51% AND is the key decision maker
  • Minimum possession of entrepreneurship/ vocational certificate
    1. Those without above could be considered through undergoing entrepreneurship training by CEDAR
  • The Company has been in operation for at least two (2) years*
    • *This requirement is waived for start-up companies under SME Bank's Umbrella

Application Method

  • Download and fill in the Application Form and send the completed form to any nearby SME Bank branch
