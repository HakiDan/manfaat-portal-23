Discount/Rebate Vouchers - Special Interest

Open for Application

Last updated on 07/10/2022

Introduction

Campaign Details
"Shop & Be Rewarded 2.0" Campaign
  • RM30 e-voucher gift for purchases as low as RM100
  • The objective is to increase the number of footfalls to the mall
"Rediscover Fun-A Theme Park Family Affair 2.0" Campaign
  • RM10 e-voucher for each entrance ticket at the Theme Park and Family Entertainment Center
  • The objective is to increase visitors to Theme Parks and Family Entertainment Centers
Stimulus Dive Packages PRE 2.0
  • Discounts/rebates are given to consumers who book scuba diving packages online on the agency portal.
  • Buyers who purchase packages priced at RM1,000 and above are eligible to receive a discount of RM400 (RM300 by Tourism Malaysia and RM100 by agents)
  • The objective is to assist scuba diving operators in Malaysia through MSDA which have been severely affected by the COVID-19 outbreak.
Smart-partnership with Zoo Negara
  • Discounted Price:
    1. Adult: RM20.00
    2. Children: RM10.00
    3. Senior Citizen: RM10.00
  • The objective is to increase number of visitors to Zoo Negara
Smart-partnership with Zoo Taiping & Night Safari
  • Discounted Price:
    1. Adult: RM10.00
    2. Children: RM5.00
    3. Senior Citizen: RM5.00
  • The objective is to increase number of visitors to Zoo Taiping & Night Safari.
Jomhomestay
  • Package purchase using the jomhomestay platform
  • Rebate offer with a minimum of 2D1M stay/activity
  • Each purchase of RM100, will get a rebate value between RM50 to RM300 depending on the purchase amount
  • Examples of rebates are as follows:
    1. Purchase RM100: Rebate RM50
    2. Purchase RM200: Rebate RM100
    3. Purchase RM300: Rebate RM150
  • The objective is to increase sales of Homestay operators and increase the number of visitors to homestays

Eligibility

  • Malaysian citizens
  • Each redeemer is allowed to redeem 1 redemption during the course of the campaign.

Application Method

Application Period Application Steps
"Shop & Be Rewarded 2.0" Campaign
  • 26 August – 18 September 2022 (Kempen Jualan Keluarga Malaysia Mega & Hari Malaysia)
  • Using the PKKM Application which is a QR Code where the redeemer has to scan the QR Code in the stores in the participating Shopping Centers on a "first come first served basis".
  • Redeemer needs to enter name, No. Identity Card, Registered Telephone No. and purchase amount to get an e-voucher of RM30
  • If eligible, the redeemer only needs to pay the balance of the purchase amount.
"Rediscover Fun-A Theme Park Family Affair 2.0" Campaign
  • TBA
  • Use the MATFA website for every purchase and redemption of tickets to Theme Parks and Family Entertainment Centers
  • Visitors need to fill in a name, No. Identity Card, Registered Telephone No. and registered email address
  • Confirmation of e-voucher purchase will be sent to the registered email
  • Redeemers will print an e-voucher and redeem at the entrance of the selected theme park or family entertainment center
Stimulus Dive Packages PRE 2.0
  • March – August 2022
  • Consumers need to browse the XTVT website and enter MyKad ID details to purchase the scuba -diving package offered.
  • Consumers who purchase packages worth RM1,000 and above are eligible to receive a discount/rebate worth RM400, which is RM300 by Tourism Malaysia and RM100 by the selling agent.
Smart-partnership with Zoo Negara
  • June – November 2022
  • Users need to have a Shopee account;
  • Users need to sign in to the Shopee platform and then make a purchase;
  • Users make payments using Shopee Pay;
  • Users obtain an e-ticket slip as proof of a successful purchase transaction;
  • Users show an e-ticket slip with My Kad/My Kid to redeem the entrance ticket at the National Zoo Ticket Counter;
  • Users can visit the National Zoo
Smart-partnership with Zoo Taiping & Night Safari
  • June – November 2022
  • Customers need to buy discount tickets at the Taiping Zoo & Night Safari ticket counter;
  • Customers need to show My Card & My Kid
  • Customers make payment at the ticket counter;
  • Customers can visit Taiping Zoo & Night Safari
Jomhomestay
  • April - July 2022 (Redemption until 30 November 2022)
  • Package purchase using the jomhomestay platform
  • Rebate offer with a minimum of 2D1M stay/activity
  • Users need to enter a name, No. Identity Card, Registered Telephone No. and address
  • Users make payments online at a price that has been deducted according to the rebate earned depending on the purchase amount
