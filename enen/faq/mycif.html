Malaysia Co-Investment Fund (MyCIF)

Open for Application

Last updated on 07/10/2022

Introduction

  • The Malaysia Co-investment Fund (MyCIF) was set up as part of Belanjawan 2019 in order to co-invest in Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) and Social Enterprises (SEs) alongside private investors via Equity Crowdfunding (ECF) and Peer-to-peer Financing (P2P) platforms.
  • The objectives of the fund are to:
    1. Establish a public-private partnership mechanism for risk capital financing.
    2. Create a more efficient and visible channel to disburse government funds to MSMEs.
    3. Crowd in private investors and spur further growth in the alternative financing markets.
  • MyCIF also runs the MyCIF SEs scheme to co-invest in P2P campaigns by MEDAC-accredited SEs.
    • MSMEs (ECF) MSMEs (P2P) SEs (P2P)
    Investment Ratio 1:4 1:4 1:1
    Maximum Investment RM 1 million RM 1 million RM500,000
    1. For 1:4 co-investment ratio, MyCIF will invest RM1 for every RM4 successfully raised from private investors on MyCIF participating platforms; and
    2. For 1:1 co-investment ratio, MyCIF will invest RM1 for every RM1 successfully raised from private investors on MyCIF participating platforms.
  • For further information, please refer to the MyCIF Website

Eligibility

  • MyCIF is open to all MSMEs who meet the requirements set forth by respective MyCIF participant's platform;
  • MyCIF is open to all SEs who
    1. Have been granted Basic SE, SE Accreditation (“SE.A”) or SE Accreditation Plus (“SE.A Plus”) status by the Ministry of Entrepreneurship Development under the Social Enterprise Accreditation program; and
    2. Meet the requirements set forth by respective MyCIF participant’s platform

Application Method

  • Start-ups and MSMEs interested to receive co-investment from MyCIF should contact any of the ECF or P2P platforms participating under the MyCIF Scheme.
