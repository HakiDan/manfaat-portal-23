SIP Gig Programme

Open for Application

Last updated on 07/10/2022

Introduction

  • SIP Gig is a financial incentive of RM600 to RM800 per month for a maximum period of 6 months for new gig employees under Budget 2022.
  • Incentive payments under the SIP Gig Programme are implemented as follows:

    • Payment Condition Monthly Commission/
    Worked Hours    		 Eligible Monthly Incentive Amount
    After submission of Activity Log by orang berkerja sendiri (OBS) (for 6 months) Earned at least RM1,200 monthly OR worked for more than 40 hours per week RM600
    Earned RM2,500 or more OR worked for more than 60 hours per week RM800
    (Additional RM200)
  • This incentive focuses on individuals who have been given notice of unpaid leave, retrenched, unemployed for more than 60 days, job seekers from the affected group and housewives who have not worked for more than six (6) months who are engaged in work that have irregular working schedule using the Digital Platform registered under SOCSO.
  • Objective of SIP Gig Programme:
    1. Provide opportunities for unemployed individuals to perform short -term employment.
    2. Encourage job seekers or unemployed individuals to take up employment opportunities across informal employment or gig economic careers through digital platforms.

Eligibility

  • Individuals who have been given notice of unpaid leave (minimum of one month) or who have been retrenched.
  • Individuals / graduates who are unemployed for more than 60 days.
  • Job seekers from the affected groups such as the disabled, ex -prisoners and others.
  • Housewives who have not worked for more than 6 months and household income is less than RM2,208 in line with the latest Poverty Line Income (PLI) limit.
  • Employees for this programme must be 18-60 years old by 2022.
  • Employees for this programme must register from 1 January 2022 on a digital platform registered with SOCSO.

Application Method

  • SIP Gig Programme Application is open from 17 January 2022 through the PERKESO Prihatin Application (PlayStore / AppleStore) or through Matrix Portal until 31 December 2022
  • Eligible Self-Employed individuals can apply for the SIP Gigi programme by:
    1. Downloading the PERKESO Prihatin Application from the PlayStore / AppleStore or visit Matrix Portal
    2. Browsing digital platforms registered with SOCSO only
    3. Applying at the SOCSO Application or at Matrix Portal
    4. If the application is approved, activity logs need to be uploaded every month to receive the incentive.
  • Mandatory documents required by Self-Employed individuals for verification in the PERKESO Prihatin application:
    • Individual's bank account information (copy of front page of bank statement containing employee's name, bank account number, identity card and bank name)
    • List of supporting letters or documents by qualification category:

    Category Documents
    SOCSO contributors who are given notice of unpaid leave or who are retrenched Evidence issued by current or previous employer
    Job seekers from the affected group registered with government related agencies Proof of verification issued by an agency registered with the government (Example: OKU Card from the Social Welfare Department)
    Vulnerable job seekers (Not registered with government agencies) Household Income and Dependent Verification Form certified by the Village Head/ Village Chief, Chairman of the Village Development and Security Committees (JKKK)/ Grade A Government Officer (Grade 41 and above).
    Housewives who have not worked for at least 6 months (Household income less than RM2,208 in line with the Poverty Line Income (PLI) limit
    Graduates who are unemployed for more than 60 days Graduation Letter that has been certified by an Institution of Higher Learning (IPT)
