Introduction

  • The JomKerja@NCER is fast-track human capital incentive implemented to support companies and businesses that are operating, hiring, investing, and expanding in NCER
  • The participating companies are provided with a human capital incentive and access to a varied talent pool comprising of fresh graduates, youths, and retrenched workers to meet their operational needs
    1. The job opportunities and training provided provide a better quality of life and ensure a skilled workforce that matches industry needs within NCER
    2. Through this programme, companies are encouraged to hire local talent who will receive the necessary hard and soft skills to enhance their competency and employability
  • NCIA contribution is capped at a maximum of RM1,000 per month per participant (Total of RM6,000 over 6 months)
  • The applicants are encouraged to provide training throughout the contribution period for the positions hired

Eligibility

  • If the company falls under the targeted sectors AND is active in hiring OR;
  • If the company is investing and expanding in NCER OR;
  • If the company has successfully participated in NCIA programmes previously, then your company is eligible to apply

  • The targeted sectors are:
    1. Manufacturing
      • Electrical & Electronics (E&E)
      • High End Manufacturing
      • Medical Devices
    2. Services
      • Logistics
      • Digital Economy
      • Tourism
    3. Agriculture & Bio-Industries
      • Smart Farming

Application Method

    Item Explanation
    Required documents
    • License and/or relevant certification relating to company activities
    • Latest 3 years financial audited statements
    • Memorandum and Articles of Association (M&A)
    • Form 9 - Certificate of Incorporation
    • Form 24 - Particulars of Allotment of Shares
    • Form 49 - Particulars in Register of Directors, Managers and Secretaries and Changes of Particulars
    • Form 13 - Change of Name (if any)
    • Recruitment Job Description and Training Plan (Training Description & Milestone)
    • Company Director's Resolution
    Application process
    • Visit the NCER Portal
    • You need to click on “Register a new account”, fill-in your valid email address and password before clicking “SUBMIT”
    • You will receive an email notification from the System to activate your Account
    • You can log-in to the system and start to fill-in your application
