Introduction

  • Be Your Own Boss (BYOB) is an initiative by Pernas that provides a holistic and competitive entrepreneurship programme. It targets bumiputera youths and women who want to venture into entrepreneurship, especially in franchise and pre-franchise businesses
  • Participants will have the opportunity to attend structured training, business matching, financing assistance and be monitored for six (6) months upon commencement of business
  • Objectives:
    • To provide financing assistance and structured training programs for women and youth whilst introducing them to entrepreneurship and franchising; and
    • To provide guidance mentoring and monitoring for participants to ensure successful ventures. A business coaching and monitoring sessions will be conducted for 6 months after the commencing of the business

Franchise Pre-Franchise

A franchise refers to a business marketing system whereby a company (franchisor) grants the right to another company (franchisee) to operate a business in accordance with the franchise system established by the franchisor during a term to be determined by the franchisor at a determined location, to distribute an existing product or services. Based on Franchise Act 1998, all business that requires a franchise status must register under the Registrar of Franchise (ROF). Businesses that involve are as follow:

  • Food & Beverages (F&B)
  • Services
  • Education
  • Retail
  • Agriculture
  • Beauty & Healthy

    Pre-franchise business or Licensing is a system whereby the company (licensor) sells their rights to the licensee which enables them to use their product for royalty. Businesses that involve are as follow:

  • Food & Beverages (F&B)
  • Services
  • Beauty & Healthy
  • Retail

    Eligibility

    • Two programmes under BYOB, SWEET and YOUNITY where participants who are interested in registering for this project must complete the qualification as stated below:
    SWEET PROGRAMME YOUNITY PROGRAMME
    Participant Criteria
    • 21 – 60 years old (women)
    • The major shareholder(s) must be women and hold a minimum of 60% shares in the company
    • 21 - 40 years old (men and women)
    • The major shareholder(s) must be youth and hold a minimum of 60% shares in the company
    Company Criteria

    Financing eligibility criteria Franchisee are as follows:

    • Entrepreneur is the owner/operator
    • Franchisor/Master Franchisee registered with Registrar of Franchise (ROF)
    • 100% equity owned by Bumiputera
    • Letter of Offer from Master Franchisor/Master Franchisee
    • Financing application through Sdn.Bhd company for financing up to RM 500,000.00. However, financing application by Sole-proprietorship, partnership, Limited Liability Partnership (LLP) are limited up to RM 250,000.00 only
    • The business must be Syariah compliant
    • Customer must have good credit record and subject to Pernas credit criteria

    Financing eligibility criteria Pre-Franchisee are as follows:

    • Entrepreneurs is the owner/operator
    • 100% equity owned by Bumiputera
    • Letter of Offer from Pre-Franchisor
    • Financing application through Sdn.Bhd company for financing up to RM 500,000. However, financing application by Sole-proprietorship, partnership, Limited Liability Partnership (LLP) are limited up to RM 250,000 only
    • The business must be Syariah compliant
    • Customer must have good credit record and subject to Pernas credit criteria
