Microplus Kampung Baru Cina Financing (PKBC)

Open for Application

Last updated on 07/10/2022

Introduction

  • This loan is specifically for businesses in the following sectors:
    1. Retail and Wholesale
    2. Manufacturing
    3. Services
  • BSN Micro PKBC is a Micro Loan Scheme, without collateral, where the objective of the loan is to help micro entrepreneurs to grow businesses specifically for the :
    1. Permanent residents of Perkampungan Baru Cina
    2. Applicant who are not resident of Kampung Baru Cina who own and operate their business within Kampung Baru Cina vicinity.

    Item Explanation
    Loan Purpose
  • Working Capital;
  • Capital Expenditure or;
  • Renovation of Business Premises
    		•
    Loan Amount
  • RM5,000 to RM50,000
    		•
    Loan Period
  • 6 months to 5 years
    • Interest rate
  • Interest rate on a monthly balance basis of 4% per annum
    		•

Eligibility

  • Micro Enterprise (Sole Proprietorship / Partnership / Private Limited Company including Professional businesses)
  • Malaysian owned business
  • Permanent resident of Perkampungan Baru Cina as confirmed by the Pegawai Kemajuan Perkampungan (PKP)
  • Registered with Suruhanjaya Syarikat Malaysia (SSM) / Local Authority (LA) / respective Professional Bodies (for Professional business)
  • Business has been in operation for at least six (6) months

Application Method

  • Required documents:
Sole Proprietorship / Partnership Private Limited Company
  • A valid Business Registration with SSM or Business License with Local Authority (LA) / Certificate of Practice (for Professional business)
  • Certified True Copy (CTC) of the followings:
    • Form 9, 24, 44, 49
    • Memorandum & Articles of Association (M&A)
Confirmation letter from the Pegawai Kemajuan Perkampungan (PKP) stating the applicant is a permanent resident of Perkampungan Baru Cina
  • A copy of NRIC of sole proprietor / partners
  • Resolution of the Board of Directors
  • Latest 2 years of Management Account
  • A copy of NRIC of all Directors / Shareholders / Guarantors (if any)
  • Latest 6 months of Company’s Bank Statement
  • Latest 2 years of Audited Accounts / Management Accounts
  • Any other supporting documents as required by the Bank
  • Latest 6 months of Company’s Bank Statement
