Perlindungan Tenang Voucher Programme (PTV)

Applications Closed

Last updated on 08/12/2022

Introduction

  • PTV is a financial assistance scheme to expand the social protection for the lower-income group
  • From 1 January 2022, an RM75 voucher is allocated to eligible Bantuan Keluarga Malaysia (BKM) recipients to purchase new Perlindungan Tenang plan or renew their existing Perlindungan Tenang protection plan
    • From 15 July 2022, eligible BKM recipients can redeem a RM75 voucher with a minimum payment of RM5.00 and enjoy the benefits of insurance protection or takaful protection that provides financial protection in the event of death, fire or any other unwanted incident.
    • Open for application until 30th November 2022

Eligibility

  • This Programme is open for eligible BKM recipients under household, single and senior citizen categories

Application Method

    To check on your eligibility:

  • Method 1 – Check Online:
    1. Go to MyPTV Portal;
    2. Key in your National Registration Identity Card (NRIC) number and tick (/) “YES, I HAVE READ AND UNDERSTOOD THE PRIVACY NOTICE” and “I’m not a robot”;
    3. Press “Search” button and if you are eligible for the plan, the PTV system will generate a voucher number in the next message.
  • Method 2 – Check via Walk-In counter:
    1. Walk-in to any branch or authorised agents/distributors of the insurers/takaful operators to check on your eligibility.

    Steps to redeem Perlindungan Tenang voucher:

  • Method 1 - Redeem via Online:
    1. Go to MyCoverage Portal
    2. Scroll down to view the “Perlindungan Tenang Products” section. Click the Perlindungan Tenang company/product logo to retrieve basic information about the plan. Please review the criteria of each plan such as age limit and amount of premium/contribution before deciding which plan best suits your needs;
    3. Click the “LEARN MORE” button and you will be taken to the website of the insurance company or takaful operator that offers the Perlindungan Tenang plan
    4. You will be guided on an easy process to purchase the specific plans. You need to fill in the required information such as name, Identity Card (NRIC) number, age, gender and others to complete the purchase process. You may also need to enter your unique Perlindungan Tenang Voucher number to claim the voucher when purchasing the plan. You are advised to read the terms and conditions of the plan before making a purchase
  • Method 2 - Redeem Via Walk-In counter:
    1. You can also redeem your voucher by participating in/purchasing a Perlindungan Tenang plan directly from the branch office or authorised agents/distributors of the participating insurers/takaful operators counters
