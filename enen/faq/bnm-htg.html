High Tech and Green Facility (HTG)

Open for Application

Last updated on 18/10/2022

Introduction

  • HTG is a financing facility designed to help SMEs and innovative start-ups to grow their businesses. This initiative also helps in investment of strategic sector and technologies fields (digital tech, green tech and biotech) for a sustainable and entrenched economic recovery.

    • Items Explanation
    Purpose of Financing
  • The objectives of this iniative include:
  • Working Capital; and/or
  • Capital Expenditure (CAPEX)
    		•
    Financing Size
  • To create:
    • Up to RM10 million
    Financing Rate to SMEs
  • To support:
    • Up to 3.5% p.a for financing without guarantee; or
    • Up to 5% p.a inclusive of guarantee fee
    Tenure
  • To increase:
    • Up to 10 years

Eligibility

Malaysian SMEs* in the following sectors:

  • Manufacturing
    • Electrical and Electronics (E&E)
    • Chemicals
    • Refined petroleum
    • Machinery and Equipment (M&E)
    • Medical devices & pharmaceuticals
    • Automotive and transport equipment (including aerospace; vehicle and parts manufacturers
  • Services
    • Information and Communication Technology (ICT)
    • Research and Development

OR

Businesses (including commercially ready start-ups) involved in providing technology solutions:

  • Green technology
    • Renewable energy & energy efficiency
    • Transportation
      • Clean transportation, electric vehicle and battery development
    • Natural resources
      • Sustainable water and waste management
      • Aquatic conservation and pollution prevention
    • Circular economy
      • Reusing, recycling and remanufacturing
      • Repurposing of stranded assets
    • Agritech
      • Precision and automated farming
      • Vertical farming
      • Farming using drones, IoT and big data analytics
    • Low carbon technologies
      • Low carbon-emitting technologies (Carbon, capture, utilisation and storage (CCUS), low carbon hydrogen)
  • Digital and 4IR technology
    • Smart manufacturing (IoT solutions)
    • Artificial Intelligence (AI)
    • Big data analytics
    • Virtual/ Augmented reality
    • Robotics
    • Drone technologies
    • Connectivity and distributed infrastructure (5G, blockchain, cloud computing)
    • 3D printing
    • Software development (digital healthcare technology, e-commerce, fintech and payment platform, gaming mobility solutions)
  • Biotechnology
    • Neurotechnology
    • Biohealth (genetics)
    • Biosciences (crop and yield improvement)

OR

  • SMEs and start-ups in key Government programmes that are involved in innovation and commercialisation for range of critical technologies such as 4IR-related technology, green technology, digital and biotechnology under national blueprints. List of eligible programmes will be updated periodically in this link
  • Nota:
    • Recipients of Special Relief Facility (SRF), PENJANA SME Financing (PSF) and/or PENJANA Tourism Financing (PTF) are eligible for the HTG initiative with the purpose of financing capital expenditure only
    • All programme participants are encouraged to receive a letter from the respective agencies to ease the verification process by PFIs
