Open for Application

Last updated on 07/10/2022

Introduction

  • There are 4 projects under the Center of Excellence For Digital Content Production, Resources, Restoration & Preservation 2.0 (COE 2.0):

    • Projects Benefit/Objective of Project
    Tactical Production Vehicle Equipment
    • Facilitating the production of government audio visual content with more efficient transportation.
    • Providing comfort and safety in the production of audio visual content as well as facilitating the production and collection of creative content materials between locations.
    • Facilitating transport links in the publication of higher quality creative content materials and meeting local and international broadcast standards further helps to achieve the national digital content production objective so that it is on the right track.
    Restoration and Preservation
    • Ease of conservation and preservation of audiovisual collections of commercial value for the reference of the public, especially local creative industry activists, researchers and students.
    • Ensure that the country's digital content are always in the best quality and can be viewed by the public;
    • Play a role in enriching the publication collection as a national treasure and reference material for future generations;
    • Ensuring that the entire production of the new high-quality audio visual collection of FINAS is capable of generating national income.
    Pro Equipment
    • Accommodating the current needs in the publication of higher quality creative content materials and meeting local and international broadcasting standards further helps to achieve the objective of national digital content production so that it is on the right track.
    • Improve the government's audio visual content through television broadcasts and social media platforms with the latest equipment.
    • Supporting facilities and strategic facilities for the development of audio visual content as well as providing conducive production and post-production facilities.
    Museology Project Training and Promotion
    • The revolutionary reference center for the film industry and collection of Malaysian creative content
    • Knowledge of the film revolution, explaining the effects and consequences of internet content abuse and fostering interest and improving the quality of the young generation's output in the digital content industry.
