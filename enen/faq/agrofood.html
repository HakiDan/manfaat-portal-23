Agrofood Value Chain Modernisation Programme

Open for Application

Last updated on 07/10/2022

Introduction

  • This program is a financing facility offered through Agrobank in line with the focus on smart and high value agriculture in the Vision of Shared Prosperity 2030 by the Ministry of Agriculture and Food Industry (MAFI) through the National Agro-Food Policy 2.0.
    1. The program emphasizes on the modernization of the agriculture sector and the food industry along the Agro-food value chain through increased use of automation and technology based on the Fourth Industrial Revolution 4.0 (4IR).
    2. Financing under this program can be given via two models which are contract farming model and non contract farming model, where the credit assessment for this application is subject to assessment by Agrobank.
  • The scope of this financing involves financing facilities under the syariah concept (Tawarruq) which involves financing for costs related to asset purchase, development costs and working capital costs for the agriculture and food industry sectors with more efficient use of 4IR -based technology and automation and in turn can help improve business/project results.
  • Among the scope of funding are as follows:
    1. Purchase of equipment or technology to drive increased production of higher quality products compared to conventional methods;
    2. Obtain technologies and systems that have been adopted by any existing project and certified results and/or;
    3. Capital to expand business scope and technology implementation must address the increasing use of 4IR - based technology and automation.

    Financing Terms Explanation
    Financing Products

    Working Capital Financing-i (MK-i)

    • To provide for operating expenses but not limited to costs for the purchase of seeds, fertilizers, maintenance and system utilities, employee salaries, overhead costs and others related to farm operations.

    Term Financing-i (TF-i)

    • To provide capital expenditure but not limited to input costs for the purchase of seeds, fertilizers, technological equipment, procurement of materials for intelligent agricultural cultivation.
    Financing Facility Maximum up to 10 years (120 months) including a maximum moratorium period of 12 months. subject to project requirements/cashflows/ and/or other needs. Also subject to the technology lifespan.
    Profit Rate Effective rate of 3.5% per annum on the balance of the financing

Eligibility

  • Age 18 (during application) to 60 years old (upon maturity of financing)
  • Individuals or non-individuals.
  • For non-individual, organisation with 51% owned by Malaysians registered under Malaysian law such as SSM or local authorities for non-individuals such as (enterprise, partnership, Sdn Bhd) and other agencies as per approved by MAFI, operated in Malaysia.
  • Must be Shariah-compliant business
  • Credit Rating to follow Bank’s current credit policy (CCRIS, CTOS including the Section E, financial analysis, etc).
  • No bankruptcy proceeding or declared bankrupt or under any judgment proceedings.
  • Scale of operation is as deemed fit for commercial purposes (the fitness of scale of operation is subject to type farming projects), if applicable.
  • The proceeds/production are intended for domestic sales or exports
  • Business activities under commercial registered space/lot as per ACT 172 (Bahagian IV Kawalan Perancangan, Seksyen 18) and/or any relevant act pertaining the usage of land for agriculture purposes.
  • Technology to be used is towards 4IR and validated by Technology Validator or Jawatankuasa Kerja MAFI.
  • Applicants had attended relevant training with technology supplier or Anchor Company or Technology Validator, if applicable.
  • The expansion of the business with the use of validated technology must be able to improve productivity of the agriculture sector.

Specific Eligibility:

Contract Farming Model:

Anchor Company:

  • Anchor company that fulfils the eligibility as per Program Berkelompok dan Ladang Kontrak-i.
  • Anchor Company must establish Strategic Alliance Agreement with the Bank prior to financing application origination by the project participants.

Entrepreneurs (project participants):

  • Entrepreneurs (project participants) had received Project Offer Letter from the Anchor Company.

Non-contract Farming Model:

Technology Provider/Developer:

  • Business has been operating for at least 3 years; and/or
  • Have business registration or distributor certification that is still valid or in force. Standard compliance or certified SIRIM/ ISO/ MyGap is an advantage.

Entrepreneurs (not tied to Anchor Company) / Anchor Company

  • Business has been operating in the same field of at least 3 years; (*If less than 3 years, proven experienced technology providers who will provide advisory services, technical assistance, and also after sales service to entrepreneurs can be considered);
  • At least produce 2 cycles of the same agri-activities based on sales record and/or relevant documentation accepted by Agrobank.

Application Method

  • Financing applications can be made at any Agrobank branch close to the applicant's business location.
  • Documents are required for the purpose of the application
    1. Completed financing facility application form.
    2. Copy of identity card of the applicant and joint applicant (for joint application).
    3. Bank savings account statement or business sales record for the last 6 months.
    4. Copy of business registration or business license for businesses involving permanent premises.
    5. Technology validation report/ verification from the technology validator / Jawatankuasa Kerja that has been appointed by MAFI.
    6. Quotation from technology providers that have been identified and validated by a technology validator.
    7. Project offer letter from the anchor company (under Contract Farming Model)
    8. Other supporting documents required under the program.
